About Me

I’m a junior at American University studying Communications, Law, Economics, and Government (CLEG). I’m passionate about bridging divides through meaningful dialogue and empowering young people to lead with empathy.

Projects & Leadership

The Acceptance Project (TAP)

Founder of TAP, a youth-led initiative launched in 2017 to foster civil discourse among students across ideological divides. TAP has grown to over 200 students across 5 schools in Pennsylvania, offering free dialogue training and leadership development.

American University Project on Civic Dialogue

Civic Dialogue Fellow since 2022. I helped launch the PCD grant program in 2023 and facilitate campus-wide discussions on challenging topics, including the “Disagree with a Professor” series.

We Are Family Foundation – Global Teen Leader

Selected as a 2021 Global Teen Leader for my work with TAP. Collaborated with international peacebuilding organizations to expand TAP’s model nationwide.
